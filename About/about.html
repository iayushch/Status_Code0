Our Mission

In todays world, we have seen that people who are in a new place or have been reffered to a city hospital after some injury or accident, they face the challenge of gathering blood for their patiente. In such a situation it is very difficult for them to arrange it. Most of the people uses premitive method to arrange blood, like they call their relative or call to their nearby hospitals to look for the blood. It is a very time consuming process, for such an emergency situation we have come up with a solution.
